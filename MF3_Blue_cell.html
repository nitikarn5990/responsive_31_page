Oxygenin™
Oxygenin is the main key ingredient in this cosmeceutical formulation.
A MFIII proprietary ingredient, it is designed to be the most effective and
efficient oxygen carrier known to science. It captures oxygen by mimicking
the function of hemoglobin. Hemoglobin is the key element for
the blood's ability to transport essential nutrients.

 

Oxygenin in MFIII BLUECELL EXTRACT AF2 (Advanced Formula 2)
works as an active transport mechanism between the cells and intercellular space.
It increases oxygen supply, resulting in stronger diffusion, which allows cell
epidermi to absorb the optimal quantity of oxygen for the metabolic process.
It helps the cell to breathe optimally and to self-regulate better, despite
a polluted environment. It is an ideal detoxification agent, which rids the skin of
carbon dioxide by enriching the skin with higher oxygen levels.

 

MFIII BLUECELL EXTRACT AF2 (Advanced Formula) does not interfere with the skin's normal function
