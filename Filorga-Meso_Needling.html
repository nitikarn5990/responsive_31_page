Dosage & Usage:


For rejuvenation :
3 sessions conducted with a gap of 15 days to 1 month
between each of them according to the skin and indications

Recommendations and Tips for Meso-Needling
- It is possible to carry out a peeling 1 week before the 1st
meso-needling session, to refine the epidermis. Do not combine
peeling and meso-needling in a single session.
- Recommend on SPF50+ screen for the entire duration of
the treatment.
- The inner side of the arms can also be treated.
Do not apply on the hands.

For Alopecia :
Meso-Needling session every 15 days for 3 months
or every month for 5 months.

* All supplements and therapies should be used under a doctor's
supervision. Please consult with your physician prior to use.
